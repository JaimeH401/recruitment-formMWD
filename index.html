Position

Active Dispatch Agent

This is not a passive role — you will be actively managing deliveries in real time throughout your shift. You will monitor delivery teams, support drivers, communicate with customers, and handle issues as they happen to ensure a smooth and on-time delivery experience.

What you'll do

→Track and support multiple delivery teams throughout the day
→Communicate with drivers and customers to provide updates and resolve issues
→Ensure proof of delivery (photos, signatures) is completed correctly
→Handle delivery exceptions (no-shows, damages, wrong items, etc.)

Work expectations

→Manage multiple delivery teams at the same time
→Stay engaged until all assigned deliveries are completed
→Multitask, make quick decisions, and follow up consistently
